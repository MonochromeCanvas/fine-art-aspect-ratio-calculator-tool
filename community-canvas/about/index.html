About The Project

A second life for archival paper from the Monochrome Canvas studio.

A public archive made from saved studio scraps

The Recycled Studio Paper Archive turns paper offcuts, test sheets, and saved scraps into a shared record of artists, clients, families, and collaborators connected to the studio.

Recycled archival paper

The cards begin as studio remnants from the printmaking process. The paper is still beautiful, still archival, and still worth using.

Artist visibility

Public credit is optional, but contributors can share a display name, social handle, website, location, and a short note so viewers can discover the artists in the archive.

Studio review

Every image is held for approval before it appears publicly. The archive is meant to be generous, welcoming, and appropriate for artists of all ages.
